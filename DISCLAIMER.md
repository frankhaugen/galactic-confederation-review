# Disclaimer

## What this is not

This is not a fan site, a wiki, or a blog. Wikis have their place, but they are for lookups, like a dictionary, or a glossary, or a database of information, but this is not that.

## What this is

A fun way to take a thousand hours of reference material for my own writing, and make it fun and easier to consume for myself and others. To be honest, mostly myself, that's why i generate audio as an "accessibility feature", so I can listen to it while I'm working on other things, and be inspired.

## Use of AI

I am very particular about the use of AI in anything creative. It is a tool, a sounding board, and a way to test ideas and concepts, discuss distasteful subject matter, and to get feedback on fictional xenosociological concepts and ideas.

I have used very little AI to write my novels, it have been used to restructure, correct my epic amounts of typos, or maybe change a characters speech patterns across the full novel, but never to write actual content, not only is that vaguly immoral, as AI is trained on human labor and content, but I write for the joy of writing, so why would I want to outsource that to an AI?

This site however, is a different story. It is a fun way to take a thousand hours of reference material for my own writing, and to share it with the world in a fun and engaging way, BUT, I have already written the content, its lots of text files describing alien species, cultures, history, technology, but that's so boring, so I got AI to take that content and make it into this fun and engaging website, with faux-academic writing through an in-unverse republication institution. It makes it more fun for me to consume.
