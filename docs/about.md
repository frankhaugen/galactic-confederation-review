# About the Review

The **Galactic Confederation Review** was chartered in 2471 to improve scholarly exchange between member polities.

The Review does not originate most scholarship. It republishes selected works from universities, fleet historical societies, technical institutes, legal journals, policy offices, professional associations, and independent scholars.

Its purpose is not consensus.

Its purpose is circulation.

## Current archive register

This public archive edition is maintained as of **review cycle 2496.212**.
Selection dates use Confederation standard ordinal notation: year followed by
cycle day. A selection marked `2496.201`, for example, entered the Review
register on the 201st day of 2496.

## Funding and independence

The Review is funded by the Galactic Confederation under a public discourse mandate. Its editorial board is independent by charter.

The Review may publish work that criticizes:

- Confederation institutions
- member polity law
- commercial practice
- military doctrine
- historical settlements
- diplomatic compromises
- prior scholarship

Publication indicates editorial relevance, not institutional approval.

## Why cited works may be absent

Most cited papers are not republished here.

A citation to a paper, report, speech, archive, or proceeding indicates that the work exists in the scholarly record of the setting. It does not mean the Review has selected that work for republication.
