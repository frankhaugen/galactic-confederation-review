## Editorial note

Selected for republication as a concise explanation of how insurance, escrow, registry compliance, and transport certification shape commercial conduct across Confederation space.

## Abstract

The Confederation governs commerce less by command than by insurability. This paper examines mandatory transport insurance as a distributed regulatory mechanism and argues that underwriters function as practical governors of interstellar trade.
