# Contributing Authors

Published authors whose work has been selected for republication in this public
mesh archive. Each entry lists institution affiliation, a short bio in the style
of a faculty or institute page, and selections held in the archive register.

Editorial staff work — including Language Desk essays — is listed on
[Editorial Content](editorial-content.md), not here.

Maintains linkage tables between stellar registry keys, charter display names, and
