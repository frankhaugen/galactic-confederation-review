# Compact and Confederation Origins Dossier

This dossier gathers selections on how the Compact and the Galactic Confederation took shape: founding debate, moral alignment, compromise language, and Earth Union institutional history as one member trajectory among many.

It is not a chronological atlas. It is a compact entry path for readers who need founding context before entering Guardianship dispute, communications policy, or contemporary Earth Union studies.
