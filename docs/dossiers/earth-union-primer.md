# Earth Union Primer Dossier

This dossier gathers selections useful for students trying to understand Earth Union as polity, culture, legal actor, and strategic force.

It is not a complete history. It is a guided entry point across institutional chronology, naming and legitimacy, welfare-state militarism, xenological encounter, strategic posture, and abolitionist legal reply where Earth Union appears as neighbor rather than abstract symbol.
