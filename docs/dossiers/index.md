# Dossiers

**Dossiers** are curated reading packets assembled after enough related selections exist in the archive. They are not fixed-period issues and they are not closed syllabi.

A dossier gathers republications from different release dates and, often, different series. It offers a guided path through a subject the Review has republished repeatedly enough to warrant institutional indexing.

Dossiers may be updated as new selections clear review. Absent works noted in a dossier introduction remain absent until rights confirmation and editorial review permit republication.
