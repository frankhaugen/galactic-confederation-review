# Editorial Content

!!! warning "Republication indulgence"
    The Galactic Confederation Review is chartered to **republish** scholarship and
    institutional writing from across member polities. It is not chartered to
    originate debate.

    Material on this page was produced by Review editorial staff. Each entry is an
    indulgence: useful, sometimes necessary, and always disclosed as Review
    work rather than independent scholarship. Republication of editorial content
    does not imply endorsement by the Confederation, any member polity, or the
    originating institutions discussed within.

The editorial board treats these pieces as **editorial notes released at archive
scale** — terminology essays, translation cautions, and reader-facing clarifications
that no external venue quite wanted to own.

They are listed here rather than on the [Contributing Authors](authors.md) page.

## Language Desk

The **Review Language Desk** maintains civic terminology notes for cross-polity
readers. Its essays are written to prevent predictable translation accidents,
especially where Earth-origin categories are mistaken for universal ones.

The Desk accepts full responsibility for its indulgences. Several cited institutional
responses and counter-briefings referenced in these essays have not been selected
for public mesh release.
