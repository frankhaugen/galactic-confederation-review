!!! editorial "Editorial introduction"
    Board decision, cycle 2496.045: **dedicate an issue to Earth Union.** Not because Earth is central to the Confederation — it is not — but because Earth-origin institutions keep appearing in arguments that pretend to be universal.

    Naming accidents, humanitarian orders in frontier service, species terminology in translation, institutional history, civic rescue machinery, external strategic ethics, and Boarding Marine history belong in one place. Readers can disagree across essays without hunting separate register cycles.

    Commissioner Ruun's Nosies essay entered the register early as institutional counterweight. Professor Sato-Klein's history and President Emeritus Vel's memoir were held as the issue's civic spine before Colonel Venn's piece closed the register.
