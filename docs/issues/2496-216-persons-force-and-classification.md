!!! editorial "Editorial introduction"
    The board closed this issue after renewed argument over persons, force, and what registries are willing to classify. Prize Court doctrine draws the vigilante line. Artificial cognition law separates capability from personhood. Threni institutional xenology offers a governance counterexample without parenthood. Earth Fleet Boarding Marine history explains rescue violence that must remain legible to victims and lawyers alike. None of these pieces resolve the argument. Together they map its terrain.
