# Review Issues

> Galactic Confederation Review · Public archive edition

The Review publishes in **issues**: themed collections of republished scholarship assembled for cross-polity discourse. Each issue carries a release cycle in Confederation standard ordinal notation and a thematic title chosen by the editorial board.

Individual articles retain their own **selection dates** — the cycle day on which they entered the Review register. Selection dates within an issue may span several cycles while the editorial board assembles a coherent collection.

## How to read an issue

Issues are not anthologies of agreement. The Review may place incompatible arguments in the same issue when the theme requires it. Editorial introductions explain assembly logic. Missing replies, annexes, and institutional responses noted in republications remain absent unless separately selected.

## Calendar note

Confederation standard ordinal notation records year and cycle day: `2496.214` denotes the 214th day of cycle year 2496. The Review uses this notation for register entries, issue release, and selection dates throughout the archive.
