# Communications and Transit

The Communications and Transit series republishes works on ansibles, message systems, transit constraints, faster-than-light operations, and the institutional consequences of distance across the Confederation.

This series is for readers who need to understand why message policy and physical transit are never separable—not for readers treating ansible as metaphor or radio with a longer range.

## What this series does not cover

Communications and Transit does not publish ship-registry standards, prize law, or Guardianship settlement history. Engineering detail on containers and liability classes appears under Standards and Infrastructure. Fleet rescue doctrine appears under Fleet and Rescue Doctrine.
