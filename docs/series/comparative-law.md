# Comparative Law

The Comparative Law series republishes works on law across member systems, with emphasis on cases where similar terms conceal incompatible institutions, enforcement regimes, or status categories.

This series is for readers who already know one legal tradition and need disciplined translation into another—not for readers seeking a single harmonized code.

## What this series does not cover

Comparative Law does not publish engineering standards, ansible protocols, or full institutional histories. Guardianship-specific abolition argument appears primarily in Guardianship Debates. Ship registry and container standards appear under Standards and Infrastructure.
