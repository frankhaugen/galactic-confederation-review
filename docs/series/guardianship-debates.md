# Guardianship Debates

The Guardianship Debates series republishes works on Guardianship, slavery, regulated unfreedom, abolitionist pressure, legal compromise, registry fraud, and the economic machinery that sustained both settlement and resistance.

This series is for readers willing to encounter founding compromise, contemporary enforcement, and abolitionist reply in the same archival track—not for readers seeking a single moral verdict from the Review.

## What this series does not cover

Guardianship Debates does not provide clinical xenology, ansible engineering, or full Earth Union welfare history. Species-level kinship material appears in Species Profiles. Container and ship-registry mechanics appear under Standards and Infrastructure.
