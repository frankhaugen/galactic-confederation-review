# Historical Summaries

The Historical Summaries series republishes student-facing institutional history, chronology, founding narratives, and long-arc explanations of how polities became what they are.

This series is for readers who need durable context before entering law, engineering, or contemporary policy argument.

## What this series does not cover

Historical Summaries does not publish primary treaty texts, technical standards, or field memoir. Narrow legal disputes, registry practice, and communications infrastructure appear under other series. Earth Union-specific material also appears in Earth Union Studies.
