# Species Profiles

The Species Profiles series republishes introductory and mid-level works on Confederation member species, with emphasis on civic biology, social structure, legal implications, and common translation errors.

This series is for readers who want species context without reading treaty law or institutional history first.

## What this series does not cover

Species Profiles does not attempt comprehensive xenological taxonomy, clinical medicine, or full legal personhood doctrine. Works on cognitive law, registry status, and comparative criminology appear under other series. Deep historical chronology belongs in Historical Summaries.
